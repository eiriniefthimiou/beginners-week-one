more about apple

Apple is an American multinational company founded by Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak in April 1976. It is mainly related to technology, as well as designing, but also developing web services, software and various electronics. Companies purpose and first product was the Apple I computer, which later evolved into other computer models. In 2007, the company presented the iPhone, a mobile phone that was a combination of three devices. More specifically, a combination of a widescreen iPod with touch control, a mobile phone and an Internet access system. A year later, Apple launched the App Store, where it provided applications for the iPhone, and later announced the iPad, a touch screen tablet computer. And later, she opened her own store.
